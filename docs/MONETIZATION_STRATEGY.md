# Windjammer Monetization Strategy

## Executive Summary

Windjammer will use an **open-core model** with multiple revenue streams to ensure long-term sustainability while maintaining community goodwill through open-source components.

**Target**: $2.3M+ ARR by Year 2

---

## Core Philosophy

### What We Believe:
1. **Open source drives adoption** - Language and UI framework must be free
2. **Professional tools deserve payment** - Game developers will pay for quality
3. **No runtime fees ever** - We compete with Unity by being better, not cheaper
4. **Sustainable development** - Revenue funds continued innovation
5. **Community first** - Free tier must be genuinely useful

## Competitive Pricing Analysis

### vs. Unity:
- **Unity Pro**: $185/month/seat
- **Unity Enterprise**: $4,000+/year
- **Runtime fees**: $0.20/install (controversial)
- **Windjammer Pro**: $99/month (cheaper)
- **Windjammer**: No runtime fees (better)

### vs. Unreal:
- **Unreal**: Free
- **Revenue share**: 5% after $1M revenue
- **Windjammer Pro**: $99/month (predictable)
- **Windjammer**: No revenue share (better for successful games)

### vs. Godot:
- **Godot**: Free & open source
- **No commercial support**: Community only
- **Windjammer Free**: Competitive
- **Windjammer Pro**: Professional support (better for studios)

**Positioning**: "Unity pricing without runtime fees, Unreal features without revenue share, Godot freedom with professional support"
