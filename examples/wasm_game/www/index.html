How to Play

  • Click on cells to toggle them alive/dead
  • Pause/Play to control the simulation
  • Step advances one generation when paused
  • Random creates a random pattern
  • Clear resets the grid

Rules: Each cell with 2-3 neighbors survives. Dead cells with exactly 3 neighbors become alive. All other cells die.
